A Few Tips to Get Started:

  1. never forget to prime or moisturize before applying any makeup to your face
  2. use a damp beauty blender to blend your foundation and concealer out smoothly
  3. if you're doing a dramatic eye look it's best to do your eyeshadow before your foundation because of fallout
  4. take your time on your eyebrows so they don't look as if they've been painted on
  5. always blend your foundation down your neck so it doesn't look unblended
  6. it's always smart to do a nuetral lip if you're doing a bold eye and vice versa
  7. use tape to achieve a perfect winged eyeliner
